Ministry Training

Equipping and Empowering Servant Leaders

At Deliverance Ministries International, we are passionate about equipping and empowering servant leaders to make a lasting impact in our church and community. Our Ministry Training programs provide practical and spiritual guidance to help individuals discover and develop their God-given gifts and talents.


Whether you're interested in leading a small group, serving in our outreach efforts, or contributing to the worship team, our Ministry Training opportunities cover a wide range of areas to suit your interests and passions.

Key Features of Our Ministry Training:

  • Interactive Workshops and Seminars
  • Biblical Teaching and Discipleship
  • Hands-On Experience and Mentorship
  • Prayer and Spiritual Growth
  • Opportunities for Service and Leadership

Making a Global Impact

International Ministries

At Deliverance Ministries International, we are committed to making a positive impact beyond our local community. Our International Ministries focus on spreading the love of Christ to people around the world.

Through partnerships, missions, and support, we have been able to provide aid, share the gospel, and contribute to the well-being of diverse communities across different countries.

Our Global Initiatives:

  • Humanitarian Aid Projects
  • Mission Trips and Outreach
  • Church Planting and Support
  • Education and Healthcare Initiatives
  • Cultural Exchange and Relationships
